### [RC Controller: RadioMaster TX16S](https://radiomasterrc.com/products/tx16s-mk3-radio-controller)
- Dimensions: 287 mm x 193 mm x 129 mm  
- Weight: 820 grams (without battery)
- 16-channel radio transmitter used by the pilot to manually control the aircraft during flight.
- Sends control inputs such as throttle, roll, pitch, and yaw to the onboard RC receiver.
- Supports multiple RF protocols and is compatible with receivers such as FrSky, ELRS, and Crossfire depending on module configuration.
- Equipped with Hall-effect gimbals for precise and smooth control inputs.
- Includes a built-in LCD display for model setup, telemetry monitoring, and system configuration.
- Displays telemetry data such as battery voltage and signal strength from the aircraft.
- Operated by the pilot on the ground and **external to the UAS airframe**.
